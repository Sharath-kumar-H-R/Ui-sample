Membership Feature – F&B POS System

Documentation

This feature allows businesses to define membership levels for customers based on their lifetime spending. Membership levels determine loyalty point rewards and redemption rules.

Auto Membership Upgrade

The system automatically assigns or upgrades a customer's membership level based on their lifetime spend. No manual action is needed. For example, if a customer spends Rs 10,000, they can automatically become a "Gold" member if configured.

Example

  • Membership: Gold
  • Lifetime Value: Rs 10,000
  • Loyalty Points Per ₹1: 10
  • Min Redemption Points: 100
  • Max Redemption Points: 500

If a customer has 200 points, they can redeem: 200 ÷ 10 = Rs 20 discount.
